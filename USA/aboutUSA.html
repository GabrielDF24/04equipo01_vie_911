About The Project

The fight against childhood anemia remains one of the main outstanding tasks of the Peruvian State, approximately 43.6% of children between 6 and 35 months of age suffer from this condition throughout the country, according to the National Institute of Statistics and Informatics (INEI), once presented the results of the Demographic and Family Health Survey (Endes), carried out last year.

According to the INEI, regions with a higher morbidity index are: Puno (75,9%), Loreto (61,5%), Ucayali (59,1%), Pasco (58%), and Madre de Dios (57,3%). And the regions with the lowest index are: Moquegua (37,2%), Tacna (37%), y Arequipa (34,2%).
